Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out. Still curious? We'd love to hear from you.

How long do projects typically take?

It depends on the service. Logo projects are typically 2–3 weeks. Brand suites run 2–8 weeks depending on the tier. Custom websites vary based on scope. Social media management is ongoing monthly. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start anything.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes — we work on a simple split: 50% to start, 25% at the midpoint, and 25% before final delivery. Monthly services like Team Up are billed at the start of each month. No surprises.

What is Team Up and what does it include?

Team Up is our full-service social media management offering. We handle everything — strategy, content creation, scheduling, community engagement, and analytics — across four monthly tiers from Freshman to Head Coach. Think of us as your in-house social team.

What's included in the free consultation?

A no-pressure call where we learn about your business, discuss your goals, and figure out what services make the most sense for where you are. No commitment required.

Do you work with businesses outside of Indiana?

Absolutely. We work with clients nationwide. Everything runs seamlessly via video calls, email, and our project workflow — location is never a barrier.
