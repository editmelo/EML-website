Got Questions?

Questions pastors
always ask us.

We don't have any website content written. Can you still help?

Yes. We'll send you a short, fill-in-the-blanks worksheet covering service times, ministries, staff intros, and your "about" story. Most churches finish it in 30 minutes. If you get stuck, we'll help you write it.

We don't have a logo — is that a problem?

Not at all. We can use clean typography in the meantime, or we can add a simple logo to your Website Plug build. If you want a full brand identity, we offer that separately starting at $3,000.

What if we don't like the first design?

You get one full round of revisions included. We share a preview link before launch so nothing goes live until you approve it. Most churches love the first draft — but we want you to love it.

Do you work with specific denominations or church sizes?

We're denominationally neutral — we've designed for faith-based organizations across the board and match your tone, not ours. The Website Plug is built for churches between 50 and 800 attendees.

Can we pay monthly instead of $200 upfront?

At this price point, we need the full $200 upfront to keep turnaround fast. For full-size brand projects ($3,000+), we offer a 50/25/25 payment split.

Do we have to use your hosting?

No. Our $25/mo hosting is optional and includes ongoing maintenance, security updates, and light edits. If you already have hosting you love, we'll hand over the code and help you deploy.

What happens after April 30?

This exact offer ends. Our standard custom website service starts at $1,500, and we'd still love to work with you — it's just a different scope. If you're on the fence, book the free call this month.
